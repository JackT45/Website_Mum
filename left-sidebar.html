About us

What we do

At Humble Beginnings we are all about creating something from nothing. We take items that would otherwise be thrown away, shoved in the loft, sent to the charity shop or car boot sale and turn them into things of beauty. In this throw away world in which we live, we want to share our message and inspire others to see the possibilities that are all around each and every one of us.

Our Process

As much as possible try not to buy new materials - use up that pot of paint that has a little bit left over from a decorating project, that piece of fabric you bought too much of when making new curtains or the ribbon, wool, string, buttons and beads you have stashed away because they are bound to come in handy one day. Visit the charity shops and car boot sales and you will be amazed what you will find that can be used in a project - fabric, wool, picture frames, clocks, clothing, maps, books, vinyl records, pretty glasses - the list is endless. At the risk of creating a space like Steptoe’s Yard, keep old glass or plastic bottles, jars, cardboard boxes and paper. Add a little imagination and flare and, before you know it, you have created something you are proud to have on display in your home.

You could be lucky enough to come across an absolute gem that a neighbour has put outside their house with a little notice saying “free to collect” - a lovely little bureau that they no longer have room for or even an egg chair that has been used and loved by the children of the family but now outgrown and is taking up too much space. Use that fabric you have had for years to make pretty cushions and curtains and turn it back into a cosy place where another child can sit and read their favourite book and enjoy it for years to come.

It maybe something that it simply beautiful to look at such as a picture that has been sat in the loft for many years having gone out of favour and given a new lease of life using the tail end of a pot of paint, some wool that was bought from a charity shop and a bit of imagination.

It could be something that is both beautiful and practical. Take some empty glass bottles, stick them together, wrap around a piece of ribbon that has been sat in the ribbon box from a previous project and maybe add a few buttons or other embellishments and you have a lovely vase. The added bonus is that these vases make flower arranging an absolute doddle.

How about a foot stool that has seen better days - a lick of paint, a piece of foam that you have left from another project and an old coffee sack (bought on a trip to Porlock from Miles Tea & Coffee Merchants which, until recently, they simply used to throw away) and suddenly you have something lovely to look at and a place to rest your weary feet after a busy day.

What we can do for you

Because of the way we work and the materials we use, all the items we create at Humble Beginnings are unique. Visit our Esty shop to see what is available at the time. We are also happy to take commissions and work with you to create the perfect piece you have been looking for. If you are local to us, why not contact us and we can bring our little shop to you - invite some friends around and you can all browse our products and have a lovely shopping experience. As a host you will receive a thank you gift and 10% commission on all items sold at your party which you can use to purchase something that has caught your eye on the day or put towards a completely unique item we create just for you.

We are so passionate about working in this way that we want to teach other people how to create this loveliness. We are pleased to offer one and two day courses when you can come along and get creative with us. You don’t have to be artistic or even creative - there is no right or wrong in the way we work. Come along and share our vision, tap into our imagination, splash a bit of paint about and have fun!.

Never forget… “From Humble Beginnings, Come Great Things”
